About Us

Vikrant D. Shetty & Associates is a law firm based in Mumbai. Being experienced with the workings of the Indian legal system, we approach matters with a practical outlook and are able to provide clients with a realistic overview of potential risks, costs and timelines that would be involved in their matters. We ensure that all documentation and advice is delivered within promised timelines, without compromising on quality and clarity.

Practice Areas

Arbitration & Commercial Litigation

  • Commercial litigation across courts & tribunals (Supreme Court, High Courts, NCLT, RERA, etc.).

  • Hands-on arbitration experience, including settlement brokering.

Insolvency & Bankruptcy (NCLT)

  • Expertise in representing creditors, debtors, insolvency professionals.

Real Estate

Tenders
